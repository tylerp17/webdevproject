The Start (1980s)

  • Mario first appeared in Donkey Kong, for the arcade, in 1981. He was known as "jumpman"
  • Super Mario Bros, the first game of the franchise, released for arcade in 1983
  • Super Mario Bros released on NES in 1985
  • Today, the original Super Mario Bros is the 9th best selling video game of all time

3D Graphics (1990s)

  • Super Mario 64 released in 1996 and was the first mario game to introduce 3D graphics
  • Super Mario 64 is the first main stream mario game to feature Charles Martinet as the voice of Mario
  • Other 3D Mario Games:
    1. Super Mario Sunshine (2002)
    2. Super Mario Galaxy (2007)
    3. Super Mario Galaxy 2 (2010)
    4. Super Mario 3D Land (2011)
    5. Super Mario 3D World (2013)
    6. Super Mario Odyssey (2017)

The Franchise Today

  • More than 600 million copies of Mario games have been sold
  • Estimated revenue above $36 billion
  • The best-selling video game franchise of all time
  • It is also the 9th highest-grossing media franchise of all time
  • Other Mario games include:
    • Paper Mario
    • Mario Kart
    • Super Smash Bros
    • Mario Party
    • Mario & Luigi
    • Dr. Mario
    • etc.
